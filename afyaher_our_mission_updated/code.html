The Meaning

Roots of AfyaHer

The word 'Afya' stems from Swahili, meaning health, wholeness, and vitality. At AfyaHer, we believe health is not just the absence of illness, but the presence of total physical, mental, and social harmony.

Founded by a collective of healthcare curators and community storytellers, AfyaHer was born from a simple realization: the digital health space for African women was either too clinical or too disconnected from our cultural nuances. We created the sanctuary we couldn't find.
