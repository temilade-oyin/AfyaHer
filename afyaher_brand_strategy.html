# AfyaHer Brand Strategy & Direction Document ## 1. Brand Overview AfyaHer means “Health for Her.” Afya means health in Swahili, and “Her” represents the femininity behind the brand. AfyaHer is an organization created to empower women to own their health, use their voices, and choose themselves unapologetically. ## 2. Mission To empower women with clarity, courage, and community so they can prioritize their health, own their identity, and choose themselves without guilt. ## 3. Vision To build a generation of women across Nigeria and Africa who feel seen, heard, and empowered to advocate for their health and wellbeing. ## 4. Core Values - **Safe Space:** Environment without judgment. - **Clarity:** Simplifying reproductive health conversations. - **Self-Ownership:** Prioritizing wellbeing. - **Honesty:** Speaking openly about silenced issues. - **Community:** Collective healing and empowerment. ## 5. Target Audience Women who feel confused about their bodies, downplay their pain, put others first, or need a safe space to be heard. Defined by experience, not age. ## 6. Core Focus Areas - Women’s Reproductive Health - Emotional Empowerment - Community Conversations - Advocacy & Awareness ## 7. Brand Personality & Tone Warm, calm, honest, and thoughtful. Validating, non-condescending, and firm but gentle. ## 8. Organizational Direction (Year 1) - Community Engagement (Small groups, story-sharing) - Outreach Programs (Reproductive health education) - Monthly Gatherings (Themed interactive spaces) ## 9. Impact Goals (3–5 Years) - Recognized safe space brand in Nigeria. - Regional reach expansion. - Consistent themed events. - Structured programs and workshops. ## 10. Long-Term Potential - Partnerships with health professionals. - Digital community platform. - Annual women's conference. - Research-driven reports.
