Frequently Asked Questions

Is AfyaHer a safe space?

Absolutely. We prioritize privacy and cultural sensitivity. Every health conversation is moderated to ensure a respectful, non-judgmental environment for all women.

How can I join the community?

By filling out the interest form above, you'll be the first to know about our upcoming regional gatherings and digital health seminars.

Do you provide medical advice?

We provide curated health information and peer support. For clinical diagnosis, we help connect you with licensed professionals in our partner network.

Can I volunteer as a student?

Yes! We have an active Student Ambassador program for those in medical, public health, or communications fields looking to make an impact.
