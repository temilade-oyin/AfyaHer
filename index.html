ABOUT AFYAHER

Health for Her.

AfyaHer means Health for Her. 'Afya' translates to health in Swahili, and 'Her' represents the femininity and power behind the brand. We aim to create a world where women are empowered to take charge of their health, use their voices, and influence decisions about their bodies, well-being, and lives. AfyaHer believes in a world where women love and respect themselves, and in doing so, teach others how to treat them. So think AfyaHer — think Health. Voice. Power.

WHAT WE OFFER

Where women come to understand their bodies.

Too many women are told they are fine when their bodies are telling them otherwise. AfyaHer exists to close that gap — giving women the clarity, community, and confidence to understand their health and advocate for themselves.

FROM THE FOUNDER

Why AfyaHer exists.

This is a calling. There has always been a deep need in me to do something for women — something personal. A need to create awareness about things society has normalised but shouldn't have. A desire to tell women that it is okay to choose themselves first. This vision began with maternal mortality. But as I dug deeper, it evolved into something broader: reproductive and women's health. I have come to understand that there is still so much friction, so much silence. And for those who do know, there is still a hush around their voices. That is why AfyaHer exists: to teach and remind women that they are their own safe space. To help them love themselves, support other women, and be unapologetic about choosing themselves — every single time.
