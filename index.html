You Are Not Alone

You were told you were fine.
You were told to wait it out.

But you are still here. Still searching. Still trying to understand what your body is telling you.

That is not weakness. That is what happens when nobody gives women real answers about their health. Most women spend years normalising symptoms, dismissing pain, and googling at 2am — not because they are not paying attention, but because nobody ever taught them how their bodies actually work.

AfyaHer exists because women deserve better answers.

From the Founder

The ovarian cyst that changed everything.

At 20 years old, I began experiencing severe abdominal pain. What followed were hospital visits, scans, medications, referrals, and confusion. Eventually, I was diagnosed with ovarian cysts.

What stayed with me most was not the diagnosis. It was realising how little I understood about what was happening inside my own body. Nobody had ever explained any of it.

Years later, after studying public health and working within women's health spaces, I discovered that what I experienced was not rare. Women everywhere were going through the same thing — confusion, dismissed symptoms, delayed diagnoses, questions without answers.

— Temilade Adeyemi, Founder of AfyaHer
